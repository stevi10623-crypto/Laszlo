An Incredible Journey

  • Roots in Hungary 🇭🇺

    Coming to us all the way from Hungary with that unforgettable heavy accent we love so much.

  • A Passion for Wine & Cognac 🍷🥂

    Mastering the art of the vine with a prestigious wine degree. And we can't forget the love for Cognac... usually accompanied by exactly 3 limes!

  • 💨 The Infinite Sneeze

    Laszlo holds the unofficial world record for the most dramatic sneeze in history. It shakes the house, startles the neighbors, and is always followed by his signature satisfied grin.

  • 🍭 The Legendary Candy Stash

    We all know he says "no sweets," yet somehow, chocolate wrappers appear like magic. He is the undisputed grandmaster of hiding candy from everyone (except himself).

  • 🥩 Master of the Grill

    Show him a piece of meat and a BBQ, and he becomes a culinary wizard. His secret ingredient? A splash of cognac and a lot of Hungarian passion.

  • 🚗 Master of the Driving Curb

    Laszlo doesn't just drive; he navigates by "feel." If a curb hasn't been kissed by his tires, did the turn even happen? His steering wheel grip is as firm as his handshake.

  • ⛳ The Golfing Legend (Self-Proclaimed)

    He hits the ball... somewhere. Usually toward a tree or a squirrel, but he does it with the focus of a pro and the style of a gentleman.

  • Building Connections 🤝

    Working as a personal secretary, always organizing and connecting with people.

  • The Bar Scene 🍻

    Opening beloved bars in Long Beach and Redondo Beach, creating spaces for joy, partying, and community.

  • Business Brilliance 💼

    Thriving as a business broker and Mergers & Acquisitions specialist.

  • A Life Well Lived 🏌️‍♂️💃🤽‍♂️

    A proud member of the House of Blues on Sunset and the Braemar Country Club. Between being a competitive water polo player, tearing up the dance floor as a ballroom dancer, and enthusiastically trying (but maybe not succeeding) at golf... life is never boring!

  • A (Terrible) Driver 🚗💥

    Let's be honest... he is a terrible driver. We are all secretly very glad he doesn't drive anymore!

  • Sweet Tooth & Quirks 🍫🤧

    He has a serious weakness for candy and chocolate wafers! But watch out—if he eats too much food, the sneezing fits begin!

  • 🧐 The "Hungarian Verdict"

    Laszlo's ultimate test for anything is if it's "Hungarian enough." From cars to coffee, if it lacks that 🇭🇺 spark, it gets the classic Laszlo wince (usually followed by a lecture on why Hungarian wine is superior).

  • 🥊 The 3 AM Water Polo Match

    He once tried to explain water polo strategies using salt and pepper shakers at a restaurant. We're still not sure who won, but the table was very well-seasoned and everyone within a 5-meter radius was a "referee."

  • A Good Laugh 📺😂

    A true fan of legendary comedy. Nothing beats watching Mr. Bean (who he thinks he looks like when concentrating), Only Fools and Horses, or Two and a Half Men for a good laugh.
